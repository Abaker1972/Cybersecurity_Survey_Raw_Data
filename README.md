# Cybersecurity and Business Dynamics – KSA Case Study

## Overview
This repository contains the dataset and supplementary materials supporting
the research paper entitled:

**“Cybersecurity and Business Dynamics: A User-Centric Case Study in the
Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.”**

The study examines the impact of cybersecurity practices on business
dynamics from a user-centric perspective, focusing on organizational trust,
operational resilience, and digital risk awareness within the Saudi context.

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset is derived from a structured survey administered to users and
professionals in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It captures perceptions related
to cybersecurity awareness, business continuity, trust, and digital practices.

### Key Dimensions
- Cybersecurity Awareness
- Business Continuity and Resilience
- User Trust and Risk Perception
- Organizational Digital Practices
